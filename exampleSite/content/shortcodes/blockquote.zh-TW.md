# Blockquote

[GitHub 風格警告框](https://docs.github.com/en/get-started/writing-on-github/getting-started-with-writing-and-formatting-on-github/basic-writing-and-formatting-syntax#alerts) 用於突出顯示重要資訊。

## 使用方式

```md
> [!NOTE]
> 你的內容
```

## 範例

```md
> [!NOTE]
> 提示資訊

> [!TIP]
> 技巧建議

> [!IMPORTANT]
> 重要資訊

> [!WARNING]
> 警告內容

> [!CAUTION]
> 注意事項
```

> [!NOTE]
> 提示資訊

> [!TIP]
> 技巧建議

> [!IMPORTANT]
> 重要資訊

> [!WARNING]

> [!CAUTION]
> 注意事項
